  • Toor Dal

  • Mustard
  • cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Coriander leaves

    • Preparation Style

  • Soak half glass dal in water for atleast 8 hrs
  • move the soaked dal to a cooker
  • fill water such that the dal is submerged
  • Chop two green chilles and add to the cooker with a pich of salt
  • Take 6 whistles (varies depending on dal quality)
  • In another kadai , heat the oil and add half tea spood mustard to it
  • Add the dal into the kadai and stir well
  • Add salt and chilli powder if necessary and heat well
